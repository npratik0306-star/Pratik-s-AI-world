🧐 What even is an AI?

Before we compare, let's understand what these things are! Click any card to learn more.

📚
They "read" everything
LLMs (Large Language Models) are trained on billions of sentences from books, websites, Wikipedia, and more. They learn how words and ideas connect together — like the world's most avid reader!
🔮
They predict what comes next
At their core, AI assistants are very powerful autocomplete — like your phone keyboard, but 1000× smarter. They predict the next word, then the next, building entire answers from scratch.
⚙️
The Transformer brain
Both ChatGPT and Claude use an architecture called the Transformer (invented by Google in 2017). It reads all words at once and figures out which words matter most — just like how your brain spots "it" means the trophy, not the suitcase.
🧩
Tokens, not words
AIs don't see whole words — they see "tokens," which are chunks of letters. "unhappy" might be split into "un" + "happy." A token is roughly ¾ of a word. Claude can process 200,000 tokens at once — that's a full novel!

💎 Claude's 5 core principles

These are the values baked into how Claude thinks and responds — kind of like its personality!

🤝
Be Helpful
Answer questions as fully and usefully as possible. No unnecessary warnings or refusing for no reason.
🛡️
Be Harmless
Avoid helping with things that could genuinely hurt people. Uses real judgment — not just panicking at scary words.
✅
Be Honest
Won't make up facts. Says "I don't know" when it doesn't. Won't flatter you with wrong answers just to seem nice.
👤
Respect Autonomy
Treats you like an intelligent person. Gives you info to make your own decisions — doesn't decide for you.
🚫
Avoid Deception
Won't pretend to be human. Won't lie about what it is or manipulate you emotionally.
